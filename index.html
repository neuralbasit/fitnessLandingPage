Training blocks

Three systems.
One rhythm.

Everything Tempo does folds back into a single number: today's right effort.

SET 01 · SENSE

Live heart-rate mapping

An optical and ECG hybrid sensor reads your pulse 50 times a second, filtering out motion noise so the number on your wrist matches what's actually happening in your chest.

SET 02 · ADAPT

Rolling recovery score

Sleep, resting heart rate, and yesterday's load feed a recovery score each morning. Tempo quietly reshapes today's targets around it — no manual logging required.

SET 03 · PACE

In-session pacing cues

A single haptic pulse on your wrist means speed up, two means ease off. You keep your eyes on the road and your rhythm on target.
